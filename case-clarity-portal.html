Before You Begin

Assessment Orientation

This evaluation includes six forensic instruments designed to examine structural case factors affecting medical care progression, recovery trajectory, and litigation risk.

What to Expect
You will complete the instruments using two cases: one active case and one previously closed case.
Case information will be entered once and applied across all six instruments.
Case information entered once will automatically populate across all six instruments. You will not be required to re-enter the same information in each tool.
Each instrument typically requires approximately 10–15 minutes to complete.
Estimated total assessment time: approximately 60–90 minutes.
Most attorneys complete the assessment over multiple sessions during the 72-hour access period.
Access Window

Important Note

The instruments do not function as simple calculators. Each tool evaluates structural elements of case progression, treatment sequencing, and recovery dynamics.
