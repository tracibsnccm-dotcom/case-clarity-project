Before You Begin — How the Recovery Risk Forecaster™ Works

The Recovery Risk Forecaster™ is a forensic, evidence-based evaluation tool designed to help attorneys identify the real-world barriers that influence recovery timelines, treatment consistency, and overall case outcomes.

Rather than providing medical advice, this tool evaluates population-level risk factors, occupational exposures, and injury-specific variables known to affect recovery progression.

You will be guided through a structured series of inputs. Each section represents a category of risk that may influence recovery stability and treatment outcomes.

Your responses generate a data-driven recovery forecast that can support:

  • Case planning
  • Negotiation strategy
  • Client communication
  • Early identification of recovery barriers

Important

  • This tool is intended for attorney use only.
  • Limited case reference information may be entered to help identify forecasts associated with a specific matter.
  • No medical records or protected health information are collected.
  • All responses are used only to generate the forecast report.

To begin the evaluation, please complete the brief access form.
