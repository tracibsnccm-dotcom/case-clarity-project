What to Expect

The assessment portal will prompt you to enter basic case information for your two selected cases. That information is used across all six forensic instruments — you will not be asked to re-enter it within each tool.

The assessment is designed to be completed over multiple sessions. Most attorneys require approximately 90–180 minutes total. Each instrument takes approximately 10–15 minutes and requires thoughtful consideration of case structure, treatment progression, and recovery factors.

Reports become available as each instrument set is completed. Once all six instruments are finished, the full comparative analysis is also released.

Your 72-hour assessment window begins at the time of your first login — not at the time this email was sent. You may log in at your convenience, but please note that the window cannot be paused or extended once started.

Temporary Storage & Deletion Notice

Case information and generated reports are stored only during the active 72-hour assessment window.

All data is permanently deleted when the window expires and cannot be retrieved.

Once the assessment window closes, reports and submitted information cannot be retrieved or restored.

Please download or print any reports you wish to retain before the access period ends.
